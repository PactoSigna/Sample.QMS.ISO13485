# Internal Audit Schedule 2026

## Scope

This schedule covers all planned internal audits for calendar year 2026. Audits are conducted in accordance with ISO 13485:2016 §8.2.2, covering key QMS processes at planned intervals to verify conformity and effectiveness.

The audit program considers:

- The status and importance of processes and areas to be audited
- Results of previous audits
- Regulatory requirements applicable to medical device QMS

## Audit Criteria

Each audit shall be conducted against:

1. **ISO 13485:2016** — Applicable clause requirements for the process area
2. **Internal SOPs** — Current approved procedures and work instructions
3. **Regulatory requirements** — FDA 21 CFR Part 820 (QSR) and applicable regional regulations
4. **Previous audit findings** — Verification of corrective action effectiveness from prior audits

### Auditor Qualifications

- All auditors are trained in ISO 19011 audit methodology
- Auditors shall not audit their own work area (independence requirement per §8.2.2)
- Lead auditors have completed at least 3 audits under supervision

### Reporting

Audit results are documented in individual audit reports (AUD-RPT-xxx) with:

- Classification of findings (observation, minor NC, major NC)
- CAPA references for nonconformances requiring corrective action
- Follow-up dates for verification of corrective actions
