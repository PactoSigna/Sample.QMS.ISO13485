# SOP-006: Management Review

> **Document Type**: Standard Operating Procedure
> **Regulatory Basis**: ISO 13485:2016 Section 5.6
> **Status**: Effective
> **Last Updated**: 2026-04-05
> **Owner**: PactoSigna Quality
> **Review Cycle**: Annual

## 1. Purpose

This procedure establishes the requirements for conducting management reviews of the Quality Management System at planned intervals to ensure its continued suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness. It defines how PactoSigna's Quality Reviews collection, quality metrics dashboard, and quality objectives tracking provide structured, data-driven inputs for management decision-making.

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies to the formal review of the entire QMS by top management, including:

- Quality policy and quality objectives
- Process performance and product conformity
- Regulatory compliance status
- Resource adequacy
- Improvement opportunities

This SOP covers both scheduled annual reviews and unscheduled reviews triggered by significant events.

## 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Management Review** | A formal evaluation of the QMS by top management, conducted at planned intervals, to assess suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness |
| **Quality Management Representative (QMR)** | The person designated by top management as responsible for QMS oversight, preparation of review inputs, and tracking of review outputs |
| **Quality Objectives** | Measurable goals related to quality, aligned with the quality policy, tracked and trended in PactoSigna |
| **Review Input** | Data and information presented to management for evaluation during the review meeting |
| **Review Output** | Decisions and actions resulting from the management review, recorded in PactoSigna |
| **Quality Review Record** | A structured document in PactoSigna's Quality Reviews collection that captures the meeting agenda, inputs, decisions, and action items |

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| **Top Management (CEO or designee)** | Conducts the management review; makes resource allocation and improvement decisions; ensures the QMS receives adequate attention and support |
| **Quality Management Representative** | Schedules reviews; prepares input data using PactoSigna dashboards and reports; distributes agenda; records minutes and action items in PactoSigna; tracks outputs to completion |
| **Department Heads** | Provide process performance data for their areas; present departmental metrics; implement assigned action items |
| **Quality** | Compiles quality metrics, CAPA trends, complaint trends, and audit results from PactoSigna; maintains the Quality Reviews collection |

## 5. Procedure

### 5.1 Review Frequency and Scheduling

1. Management reviews shall be conducted at least annually, with the primary review scheduled in Q1.
2. Additional reviews shall be convened after significant events, including:
   - Major nonconformances or field safety corrective actions
   - Regulatory authority inspection findings
   - Product recalls or advisory notices
   - Significant organizational changes (acquisitions, restructuring)
   - Significant regulatory changes affecting the QMS scope
3. The QMR shall maintain the review schedule and distribute calendar invitations at least 30 days before the planned review date.

### 5.2 Required Attendees

| Attendee | Role in Review |
| --- | --- |
| CEO or designee | Chairs the review; makes final decisions on resources and priorities |
| Quality Management Representative | Presents compiled input data; records decisions and actions |
| Engineering Lead | Presents design and development metrics, software quality trends |
| Quality Lead | Presents CAPA, complaint, and audit trends |
| Regulatory Lead | Presents regulatory landscape changes and compliance status |

Optional attendees may include clinical, security, and operations leads as determined by the agenda.

### 5.3 Review Inputs (ISO 13485 Section 5.6.2)

The QMR shall prepare the following data from PactoSigna and other organizational sources. All required inputs per ISO 13485 Section 5.6.2 shall be addressed:

| # | Required Input | PactoSigna Data Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Results of audits | PactoSigna audit report documents and finding records |
| 2 | Customer feedback | PactoSigna Quality Events -- Complaint trends and resolution metrics |
| 3 | Process performance and product conformity | PactoSigna quality metrics dashboard -- release quality, defect trends |
| 4 | Status of preventive and corrective actions | PactoSigna Quality Events -- CAPA status summary, aging, effectiveness |
| 5 | Follow-up actions from previous management reviews | PactoSigna Quality Reviews -- previous action item status |
| 6 | Changes that could affect the QMS | Regulatory landscape updates, organizational changes |
| 7 | Recommendations for improvement | Compiled from CAPA trends, audit observations, stakeholder feedback |
| 8 | New or revised regulatory requirements | Regulatory intelligence updates and gap analysis |
| 9 | Training compliance status | PactoSigna training dashboard -- completion rates, overdue tasks, competency gaps |
| 10 | Post-market surveillance data | PactoSigna Quality Events -- complaint trends, field performance data |
| 11 | Supplier performance | PactoSigna supplier documents -- evaluation scores, nonconformance history |
| 12 | Risk management review | PactoSigna risk documents -- residual risk acceptability, new hazards |
| 13 | Quality objectives status | PactoSigna Quality Objectives -- progress against targets, trending |

### 5.4 Preparation and Distribution

1. The QMR shall compile input data into a management review agenda document at least **10 business days** before the scheduled review.
2. In PactoSigna, the QMR shall create a Quality Review record in the Quality Reviews collection with the review date and planned agenda items.
3. The agenda and supporting data shall be distributed to all required attendees at least **5 business days** before the review.
4. Attendees shall review the materials prior to the meeting and prepare questions or discussion points.

### 5.5 Conducting the Review

1. The CEO or designee shall chair the management review meeting.
2. Each input area shall be presented and discussed. The QMR shall record:
   - Key findings and discussion points
   - Decisions made by management
   - Action items with assigned owners and due dates
3. For each decision, the rationale shall be documented.
4. PactoSigna's quality metrics dashboard may be displayed live during the review for real-time data exploration.

### 5.6 Review Outputs (ISO 13485 Section 5.6.3)

Management review outputs shall include decisions and actions related to:

| Output Category | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| QMS improvement | Process changes, new SOPs, updated work instructions |
| Product improvement | Design changes, feature enhancements, usability improvements |
| Resource needs | Personnel, infrastructure, training, tooling |
| Regulatory compliance | Gap remediation, new submissions, standard adoption |
| Quality objectives | New objectives, revised targets, retired objectives |

### 5.7 Recording and Distribution

1. The QMR shall record all review outputs in the PactoSigna Quality Review record within **5 business days** of the review.
2. The completed Quality Review record shall include:
   - Attendees list
   - Each input area reviewed with summary findings
   - Decisions and rationale
   - Action items with owners and due dates
3. The meeting minutes shall be distributed to all attendees and relevant stakeholders within **5 business days**.
4. PactoSigna's audit trail records the creation and modification of Quality Review records.

### 5.8 Action Item Tracking

1. Action items from management reviews shall be tracked in PactoSigna with assigned owners and due dates.
2. The QMR shall monitor progress and escalate overdue items.
3. Status of previous review action items shall be a standing input for the next management review (input #5 above).
4. Completed action items require documented evidence of completion.

### 5.9 Quality Objectives

1. Quality objectives shall be established and maintained in PactoSigna's Quality Objectives collection.
2. Each objective shall define:
   - Measurable target
   - Measurement method and data source
   - Target date
   - Responsible owner
3. Progress against objectives shall be tracked and trended in PactoSigna's quality metrics dashboard.
4. During management review, objectives shall be evaluated for relevance and achievement. New objectives may be established, and existing objectives may be revised or retired.
